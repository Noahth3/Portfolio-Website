Overview

This project aims to elevate my professional presence with a user-friendly interface and engaging design. The core features include:

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easily navigate through showcased work with a clear layout.
  • Responsive Design: Seamless experience across all devices, ensuring accessibility for every device.
  • Darkmode Feature: Toggle between light and dark themes to enhance user comfort and personalization.
  • SEO Optimization: Improved visibility in search engines with essential metadata.

  • Engaging User Experience: Dynamic interactions through intersection observers make your portfolio stand out.
